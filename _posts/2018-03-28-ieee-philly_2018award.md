Prof. Ioannis Savidis wins the 2018 IEEE Philadelphia Section Delaware Valley Young Electrical Engineer of the Year Award. Citation: “For contributions in hardware security and trust. Specifically, for the obfuscation of digital and analog circuits through novel algorithms and methodologies that secure intellectual property from reverse engineering, cloning, and counterfeiting.”
