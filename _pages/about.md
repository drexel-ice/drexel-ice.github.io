The **Integrated Circuits and Electronics (ICE) Design and Analysis Laboratory** advances state of the art high performance and mobile computing platforms through research on circuit techniques and methodologies that target:

1. minimizing power consumption in digital and mixed signal circuits,
2. providing work-load aware on-chip power management,
3. power and clocking for heterogeneous 3-D systems, and
4. securing integrated circuits from hardware Trojans and IP theft.

The ICE lab is located in the Bossone Research Enterprise Building on Drexel's Main Campus in Philadelphia.

## Research Focus

Our work spans **3-D integrated circuits**, **hardware security and trust**, and **sub/near-threshold circuits with current-mode logic**. [Explore our research themes]({{ '/research/' | relative_url }}) and [active projects]({{ '/projects/' | relative_url }}).

## Join Us

We welcome motivated **PhD students**, **MS students**, and **undergraduate researchers** interested in VLSI design, hardware security, and energy-efficient computing. [Contact us]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) to learn more.
