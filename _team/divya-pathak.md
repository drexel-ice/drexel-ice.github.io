Dr. Divy Pathak received the M.S. degree in Computer Engineering in 2015 and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering in 2018 from Drexel University. She is the recipient of the 2017 IEEE Circuits and Systems Pre-Doctoral Scholarship. Dr. Pathak is the co-inventor in two US Patents and four patent applications. She was a Research Scientist with Indian Space Research Organization from 2003 to 2006. She was a Staff Engineer with STMicroelectronics from 2006 to 2013. She was an Intern with the IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center from 2015 to 2016. Her current research interests include circuits, systems, and micro-architecture design for post-Moore computing. Her Ph.D. thesis focuses on SMART on-chip power delivery for energy efficient computing.
