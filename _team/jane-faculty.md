Dr. Jane Faculty is an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at Drexel University and the Director of the ICE Lab. Her research focuses on high-performance analog and mixed-signal integrated circuit design.

**Education:**
- Ph.D., Electrical Engineering, MIT, 2015
- M.S., Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, 2011
- B.S., Electrical Engineering, UC Berkeley, 2009

**Research Interests:**
- High-speed data converters
- Low-power analog circuit design
- Sensor interface circuits
