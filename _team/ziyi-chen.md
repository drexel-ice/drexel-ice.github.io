Ziyi is a Ph.D. student working in the ICE lab. His research interests include post-fabrication tuning, side-channel attack on the analog circuit, and analog obfuscation with FPAA. Ziyi received his Bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, in 2017 and received his Master degree from Northeastern University, Boston, in 2019.
