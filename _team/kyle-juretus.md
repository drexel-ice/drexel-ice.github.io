Kyle Juretus received his Ph.D. from Drexel University and is now an Assistant Professor at Villanova University. His research focuses on VLSI circuit design and hardware security, including circuit-level techniques to prevent intellectual property theft and counterfeiting, mitigating side-channel leakage, and design automation for hardware security. He was an NDSEG fellow and received Bachelor of Science degrees in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering from Drexel University in 2014.
