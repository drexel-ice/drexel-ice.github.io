Vaibhav is a Research Engineer at Kioxia. Vaibhav received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. degree in computer engineering from Drexel University, PA, USA, in 2017 and 2024, respectively. His Ph.D. work focused on circuit level techniques to protect analog IP from intellectual property theft and counterfeiting. Vaibhav also explored variation aware automation of analog circuit design to reduce design time by minimizing iterations. He has experiences in designing analog and custom integrated circuits and algorithm development for EDA tools.
