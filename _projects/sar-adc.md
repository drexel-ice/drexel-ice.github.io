This project develops a high-speed successive approximation register (SAR) analog-to-digital converter targeting 5G wireless receiver applications. The design exploits time-interleaving and background calibration to achieve 12-bit resolution at 1 GS/s sampling rate in 28nm CMOS technology.

**Funding:** NSF CAREER Award  
**Students:** Alex Chen  
**Status:** Tape-out scheduled Q2 2027
