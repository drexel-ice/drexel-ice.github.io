This project developed cryogenic CMOS amplifier circuits operating at 4 Kelvin for readout of superconducting quantum bits. The designs achieved record-low noise performance while maintaining stable operation across the full temperature range from 300K to 4K.

**Funding:** DOE  
**Alumni:** Dr. Morgan Park  
**Outcome:** 3 IEEE journal papers, 1 patent filed
