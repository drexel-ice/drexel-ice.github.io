Development of a fully integrated 28 GHz phased-array beamforming transceiver IC for 5G base station applications. The design features 4 transmit/receive channels with on-chip phase shifters and power combining.

**Funding:** Industry collaboration  
**Students:** Jordan Lee  
**Status:** Schematic design phase
